#### Black Sage
+ Scientific Name: _Salvia mellifera_
+ Woody shrub
+ Sage or Mint family (Lamiaceae)
+ Drought deciduous - no leaves during the summer
+ Square stems
+ Scented leaves
+ Found in Coastal scrub
+ Two Leaves per node
